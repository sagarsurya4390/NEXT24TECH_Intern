Data Science Projects

1) "Sentiment Analysis On President Speech"

Sentiment analysis on President speeches involves the use of natural language processing (NLP) techniques to analyze the overall sentiment expressed within a speech. This analysis aims to understand the emotional tone and attitudes conveyed by the President's words.

2) "Predictive Analysis of House Prices"

Predicting house rates in Bombay (now Mumbai) involves utilizing various factors such as location, property size, amenities, market trends, and economic indicators. Data-driven approaches, including machine learning algorithms, can be employed for accurate predictions.

3) "Sentiment Analysis On Reasearch Paper Reviews"

In this project, I applied sentiment analysis techniques to evaluate reviews of research papers, aiming to understand the opinions and emotions expressed in textual data. I managed data preprocessing, implemented machine learning classifiers such as SVM, Naive Bayes, and Random Forest, and leveraged the VADER sentiment analyzer for comparative analysis. The results demonstrated the effectiveness of these methods in predicting sentiment polarity, providing insights into sentiment distribution, review confidence correlation, and classifier impact on accuracy.
